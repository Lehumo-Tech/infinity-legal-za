Platform Overview

The Infinity Legal Platform is a comprehensive legal tech SaaS designed for the South African market. It combines AI-powered legal intake, member management, attorney portals, and a lead intelligence engine into a unified enterprise-grade platform.

Portal System

Page 5

Authentication & Login

Secure login with demo credential quick-fill for client presentations. Three distinct portals: Member, Staff (Attorney), and Admin.

Three-Portal Architecture

Member Portal — Members track requests, view case status, manage plans, and communicate with attorneys.
Staff Portal — Attorneys manage assigned cases, review intakes, track deadlines, and access the lead intelligence engine.
Admin Portal — Full system oversight: revenue analytics, member management, staff performance, and system settings.
